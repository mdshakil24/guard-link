How it works?

GuardLink operates through two harmonious components: the Guardian, a compact device carried by the driver, and the Protector, seamlessly integrated into your truck's system. The Guardian's proximity sensor activates the Protector to lock the vehicle's gear as soon as you step six feet away, ensuring your truck remains immobile in your absence. Upon your return, the system disengages, allowing for smooth continuation of your journey.
